### ✨ The Arcade Features

* **🎮 Nostalgia Engine:** An infinite, auto-scaling cosmos with fluid particle generation and neon-glow physics. 
* **🎵 Synthwave Injection:** A heavy *Dreamwave Overdrive* audio track drops the exact second you deploy, locking in the late-night arcade aesthetic.
* **🔥 Savage AI Roasts:** Crash your ship, and the system literally mocks your gaming skills with a randomized array of pure disrespect.
* **📱 Cross-Platform Drift:** 1:1 fluid tracking. Use your mouse crosshair on desktop, or drag to dodge on mobile. The canvas reads your touch instantly.

---

### 💻 The Tech Stack (How it was cooked)

* **Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS:** Pure code. No heavy frameworks. Maximum performance and aesthetic.
* **HTML5 Canvas 2D API:** The math engine plotting every single laser, particle array, and neon explosion at a buttery 60FPS using `requestAnimationFrame`.
* **Dynamic Hitbox Engine:** Custom collision logic (`Math.hypot`) constantly calculating the exact distance between your lasers and the falling debris.
